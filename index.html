The houses that define luxury.

A curated index of the world's most storied fashion maisons — their ateliers, their heritage, their current collections.

Maisons

  • Louis Vuitton 🇫🇷

    Paris · Founded 1854

    Malletier to French society; the monogram canvas that became luxury's universal language.

  • Chanel 🇫🇷

    Paris · Founded 1910

    Tweed, pearls, and the codes that shaped modern womenswear.

  • Hermès 🇫🇷

    Paris · Founded 1837

    From equestrian leather to the silk carré and the Birkin — artisanal precision for nearly two centuries.

  • Gucci 🇮🇹

    Florence · Founded 1921

    Tuscan leather craft paired with a rotating cast of provocateur creative directors.

  • Prada 🇮🇹

    Milan · Founded 1913

    Intellectual minimalism and a defining voice in contemporary fashion.

  • Dior 🇫🇷

    Paris · Founded 1946

    The New Look redrew the post-war silhouette; the maison still sets the tone of couture.

  • Armani 🇮🇹

    Milan · Founded 1975

    Tailoring rooted in unstructured elegance and understatement.

    Read more →

  • Versace 🇮🇹

    Milan · Founded 1978

    Baroque opulence, the Medusa insignia, and unapologetic Italian glamour.

  • Balenciaga 🇪🇸

    San Sebastián · Founded 1917

    Architectural silhouettes from the couturier's couturier — reborn as conceptual streetwear.

  • Saint Laurent 🇫🇷

    Paris · Founded 1961

    Le Smoking, the safari jacket, the Mondrian dress — modernism translated into couture.

  • Fendi 🇮🇹

    Rome · Founded 1925

    Rome's house of fur and craft — the Baguette and the double-F emblem.

  • Burberry 🇬🇧

    London · Founded 1856

    The trench coat, gabardine, and the check — British outerwear distilled.

  • Bottega Veneta 🇮🇹

    Vicenza · Founded 1966

    Quiet luxury, no logo, and the unmistakable intrecciato leather weave.

  • Valentino 🇮🇹

    Rome · Founded 1960

    Valentino red and a lifelong devotion to romantic couture.

  • Givenchy 🇫🇷

    Paris · Founded 1952

    Minimalist Parisian elegance, forever linked to Audrey Hepburn.

  • Dolce & Gabbana 🇮🇹

    Milan · Founded 1985

    Sicilian romance, baroque ornament, and theatrical couture.
